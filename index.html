About Coco's

Since 1998, Coco's has been making top tier chocolate ranked at the top of the world,which started from a couple of chocolate connoisseurs from the traditional chocolate making families in Belgium come together to bring you the ultimate chocolate experience. Fermented and dried cocoa beans are refined to a roasted nib by winnowing and roasting. Then, they will be heated and will melt into chocolate liquor. And finally the blending process of the chocolate liquor with sugar and milk to add flavour.
